🧠 Teen Mental Health & Digital Habits Analysis 📱📊

An insightful data analysis project using Excel to explore the impact of social media, screen time, and digital habits on the mental well-being of teenagers.

🛠️ Key Skills Used:
Data Cleaning: Cleaned and prepared a complex dataset focusing on mental health indicators like stress, anxiety, and sleep patterns.
Pivot Tables & Statistical Modeling: Used advanced Pivot Tables to calculate averages and identify significant trends across different demographics.
Interactive Visualization: Designed a dynamic dashboard with a variety of charts (Column, Line, and Bar) to tell a clear story with the data.
Dynamic Slicers: Integrated interactive filters for "Age" and "Gender", allowing users to explore specific data points in real-time.
💡 Key Insights:
Digital Well-being: Analyzed how different platforms (TikTok, Instagram, etc.) affect addiction levels among teens.
The Sleep Gap: Visualized the direct negative correlation between late-night screen time and actual hours of sleep.
Mental Health Trends: Compared stress and anxiety levels between genders, uncovering key psychological patterns in digital consumption.
